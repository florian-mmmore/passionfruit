## Your brand, your story

A strong brand is more than a logo. It is the promise you make to your customers — and the story you tell. This workshop gives you the tools to articulate that story clearly.

### Schedule

**Morning: Analysis & Positioning**

- Brand audit: Where do you stand today?
- Audience work: Who do you really want to reach?
- Differentiation: What makes you unique?

**Afternoon: Implementation & Feedback**

- Developing your brand voice
- Creating a simple brand guide
- Peer feedback in small groups

### What you take home

By the end of the day you will leave with a completed one-pager of your brand strategy and concrete next steps. Materials and lunch are included.

Places are limited to 15 participants to ensure focused, hands-on work.
