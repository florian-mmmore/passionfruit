## Two days. One community. Countless ideas.

The Greenleaf Digital Conference brings together founders, developers, designers and marketing professionals to talk about the future of digital work.

### Programme highlights

**Day 1 — Wednesday, 19 November**

- Keynote: The next wave of AI integration on the web
- Workshop sessions on Astro, Tailwind and headless CMS
- Fireside Chat: From freelancer to agency

**Day 2 — Thursday, 20 November**

- Deep Dive: Accessibility as a competitive advantage
- Panel: Bilingual websites — why EN+DE should be the standard
- Closing networking with speakers and attendees

### Hybrid format

All keynotes and panels will be live-streamed. As a remote participant you get access to the virtual networking space and all recordings after the conference.

Early-bird tickets are available until 1 September.
