## Our Story

Greenleaf Digital was founded on a simple idea: professional web development should be accessible to everyone. We believe modern technologies and AI-powered workflows are changing the way websites are built.

## Our Values

- **Quality over quantity** — Every project deserves the highest care
- **Transparency** — Open communication at every project step
- **Innovation** — We use the latest technologies for the best results
- **Accessibility** — Our websites are usable for everyone
