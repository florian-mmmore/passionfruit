## Your Role

As Marketing Designer at Greenleaf Digital, you own the visual communication of our brand. You create graphics for social media, blog articles, presentations, and our website — always aligned with our brand values and design system.

The position is planned for 20–30 hours per week and is fully remote.

## Responsibilities

- Create social media graphics, infographics, and marketing collateral
- Maintain and evolve our visual design system in Figma
- Support the web team with landing page layouts and blog hero images
- Develop presentation templates for client-facing decks
- Collaborate closely with our content team for consistent visual storytelling

## Requirements

- Degree or equivalent training in design, communications, or a related field
- Strong proficiency in Figma
- Portfolio showcasing branding and/or marketing projects
- Keen eye for current design trends and clean typography
- Self-directed and structured working style in a remote environment

## What We Offer

Flexible hours, full remote freedom, and a creative team that values your ideas. We offer fair compensation and genuine creative latitude — real design work, not template-filling.
