## Your Role

As a Senior Frontend Developer at Greenleaf Digital, you are the technical anchor of our client projects. You translate designs pixel-perfectly into code, bring performance expertise to the table, and raise the quality bar for the whole team through code reviews and close collaboration.

You'll work hand-in-hand with our designers and backend developers, actively ensuring our projects stay at the cutting edge.

## Responsibilities

- Build and maintain modern, accessible web applications with Astro and TypeScript
- Collaborate closely with the design team to translate Figma designs into production-ready components
- Conduct code reviews and mentor junior developers
- Analyze and optimize performance (Core Web Vitals, Lighthouse)
- Co-own our component library and frontend standards
- Actively participate in technical discussions and architecture decisions

## Requirements

- At least 4 years of experience in frontend development
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript and modern CSS
- Experience with Astro, Next.js, or a comparable framework
- Solid understanding of web performance, Core Web Vitals, and accessibility
- Experience with design systems and component libraries (Tailwind CSS is a plus)
- Fluent English; German is a plus

## What We Offer

We offer a motivated team, exciting projects with plenty of creative freedom, and a modern remote-first work culture. Regular team events in Berlin, 30 days of paid vacation, and a generous learning budget round out the package.
