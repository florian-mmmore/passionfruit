## A New Era of Web Development

At Greenleaf Digital, we believe anyone can build a professional web project. With modern technologies and AI-powered workflows, we make web development accessible — for everyone.

### Why We're Different

Instead of relying on outdated content management systems, we use Astro, Tailwind CSS, and TypeScript. The result: blazing-fast, secure, and maintainable websites.

### Our Vision

We want every business — no matter how big or small — to have a website that delights. Not tomorrow, but today.
