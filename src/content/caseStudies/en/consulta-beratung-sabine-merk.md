Consulta Beratung is an independent management consultancy focused on strategy and organisational development. The partnership had existed for years, but their website was outdated and did not reflect the actual quality of their advisory work.

Greenleaf Digital first developed a clear content positioning: what sets Consulta apart from large consulting firms? The answer — personal guidance, long-term client relationships and measurable results — fed directly into the copy, structure and design of the new website.

The result is a website that builds trust before the first conversation takes place. Sabine Merk: "Several new mandates can be traced directly back to our new website. The investment has paid for itself many times over."
