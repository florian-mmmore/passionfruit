Thomas Vogler runs Metallbau Vogler in the second generation. The company fabricates custom steel and aluminium structures for commercial and industrial clients. Despite excellent craftsmanship, the business had long lacked a professional online presence.

Greenleaf Digital built a new website that puts the company's strengths front and centre: project galleries, clear service descriptions and a simple contact form. A local SEO strategy was also implemented, making the business easier to find in its region.

Six months after launch, between eight and twelve qualified enquiries arrive through the website each month — a direct result that delights Thomas Vogler: "For a trade business like ours, this was a real game-changer."
